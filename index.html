My Wordle

Can you guess a five letter word?

Goal:
Guess the 5-letter target word within 6 attempts.
Feedback:
Correct letter in the correct position: Green.
Correct letter in the wrong position: Yellow.
Incorrect letter: Gray.
Input:
Each guess must be a valid 5-letter word.
Strategy:
Use feedback from each guess to refine your next word.
Win/Loss:
You win if you guess the word within 6 tries; otherwise, you lose.
